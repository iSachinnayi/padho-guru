# पढ़ो गुरु — Privacy Policy
## Last Updated: June 22, 2026

### 1. Introduction
Welcome to पढ़ो गुरु ("Padho Guru"), an AI-powered NCERT study app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

### 2. Information We Collect
- **Phone Number**: Used for OTP-based login (via Firebase Authentication)
- **Photos**: Images you capture of textbook questions are temporarily processed and deleted
- **Usage Data**: Questions asked, answers viewed, streaks, bookmarks
- **Device Info**: App version, device model (for analytics & crash reporting)

### 3. How We Use Your Information
- To provide AI-powered answers to your questions
- To track your study progress and streaks
- To improve our AI models and app experience
- To send push notifications (if enabled)

### 4. AI & Data Processing
- Questions are processed via OpenAI API to generate answers
- Frequently asked questions are cached to reduce costs
- We do NOT use your personal data to train OpenAI models
- Images are deleted immediately after processing

### 5. Data Storage
- User data stored securely on Firebase Cloud Firestore (asia-south1, Mumbai)
- Offline chapter downloads stored locally on your device
- We encrypt data in transit using HTTPS

### 6. Third-Party Services
- **Firebase** (Google): Authentication, Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics
- **OpenAI**: AI answer generation (no personal data shared)
- **Google Play / Apple App Store**: Payments via in-app purchase

### 7. Your Rights
- Access your data anytime from Profile screen
- Delete your account — email us at hello@tradersguide.online
- Opt-out of notifications in Settings
- Request data deletion within 30 days

### 8. Children's Privacy
This app is designed for students aged 11-18 (Class 6-12). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.
